Our services

We provide state of the art services to meet human demands

Real Estate

After building bespoke and luxury Real Estate spanning over 17 years, Geodata Nigeria has moved from just building beautiful homes, but homes looking to the future. At the core of what we do now, we take climate change very seriously, we build eco-friendly homes, we use low energy services capable of adopting alternate energy sources like solar. Our smart and automated homes are built to enhance security and comfort, whilst keeping the entire home safe for toddler, kids, adults and the aged.

Agriculture

We have since come to the realization that a sustainable future begins with ensuring the future of food in a fast developing world. With several suspicions arising in many quarters about the non safety of the inorganic food products on shelves today, Geodata farms has taken a big leap towards the cultivation and safe processing of Africas most staple food crops such as Maize, Millet, Rice, Beans , Tubers, as well as several other fruits. This is asides the huge poultry and fishery investments kept and harvested in the most organic ways.

Mining

Our flagship mining company Golden Rock limited incorporated in year 2000 led to several inroads into the prospecting and mining of minerals such as Baryte, Gypsum, Magnetite, Wolfamite among many others. At the moment, the company has its sights steady on Gold, Coal and natural gases.

information Technology

Geodata Communication was created and incorporated in the year 2017 to cater to the diverse needs of Geodata World Services as regards to Information Technology. When the department however brought onboard some of the most tech savvy professionals in the industry, and also having kept Geodata at the fore of all its communication endeavours, it became necessary to fully explore the potentials of information technology in a world that is fast becoming technology reliant. Today, Geodata Comms is not just a company offering high end tech services, but also tailor fit Marketing Communications which bursts its patrons to profitability through a mix of well researched consumer insights research leveraged to plan and execute on target campaigns.
